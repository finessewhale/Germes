About Us
ABOUT

GeRMeS is a young team of talented enthusiasts from Odessa. George, Roman, Misha, Sergey and founder Constantin. Abbreviation: GeRMeS©

Truly, we are completely different people, with our own views on life, interests and hobbies. But we are united by a single goal that motivates us to create our NFTs through figurative understanding of reality in the process of expressing the inner and outer world. On the scale of the entire human society, art is a special way of cognize and reflecting reality. One of the forms of artistic activity of public consciousness and part of the spiritual culture of mankind, a diverse result of the creative activity of all generations.

Due to this, we develop ourselves, as well as improve the world around us. By purchasing our NFTs, you not only help a young team develop, but also become a philanthropist, because 38% of our income goes to the development of social projects. Let's change the world together!!!

Whitelist
WHITELIST

The task we are solving is related to the catastrophe caused by the global pandemic and the war in our country. Today, a huge number of people are in dire need of a comprehensive psychological, physical, and energy rehabilitation of the body as a single system. The quality of our life depends directly on health. For this, we already have specialists and technologies, but we need a center where we can combine everything into one system - "Health Center". Another important element of health is natural food. We have experience in this direction and specialists, whose products you can purchase in Ecomarket, as well as offer your services and products.

Surely many have heard the expression: "My home, my fortress." But not many of you have heard of the term "biopositive material". Eco-building is also one of the elements of our ecosystem. The shape of the house in the form of a dome has many advantages. The main ones are: energy saving (respectively saving your resources), and restoration of the human biofield.

If you are a conscious inhabitant of planet Earth, and wish to become an impulse of the evolutionary process, then you have the opportunity to become part of our community
